box-align:

The box-align property specifies how to align the child elements of a box.

語法：box-align:start|end|center|baseline|stretch;

ValueDescription
startFor normal direction boxes, the top edge of each child is placed along the top of the box. For reverse direction boxes, the bottom edge of each child is placed along the bottom of the box
endFor normal direction boxes, the bottom edge of each child is placed along the bottom of the box. For reverse direction boxes, the top edge of each child is placed along the top of the box
centerAny extra space is divided evenly, with half placed above the child and the other half placed below the child
baselineIf box-orient is inline-axis or horizontal, all children are placed with their baselines aligned
stretchThe child elements are stretched to fill the containing block
